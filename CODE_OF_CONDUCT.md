# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the project

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling or derogatory comments
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate professionally

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue
with the label `conduct` or by contacting the maintainer directly via GitHub.
